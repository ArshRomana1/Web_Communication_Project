About Pure Aesthetics



Drawing from both Eastern and Western modalities, the educational emphasis at Pure Aesthetics is on healthful lifestyles, the interdependence of health and beauty, and ecological responsibility. Our approach teaches our students to enhance their personal well-being and gives each of them a greater depth of knowledge from which to work and succeed in the skincare and massage industry. Our students will learn the latest trends and techniques along with basic skills from accomplished professionals with practical experience within the industry. Offered in a positive, creative atmosphere, the training will encourage personal growth and produce qualified, employment-ready graduates.



Our Mission

Pure Aesthetics Natural Skincare School provides a high-quality skincare and massage therapy education based upon an integrated wellness approach. Our students learn the latest trends and techniques along with basic skills in a spa-like setting from accomplished professionals with practical experience within the industry. Offered in a positive and creative atmosphere, the training encourages personal growth and produces qualified, employment-ready graduates.

Our Holistic Philosophy



We believe what you put onto your body is as important as what you put into your body. As a student, your practice will be grounded in anatomy and physiology, and you will develop your skills using organic, small-batch and locally produced skincare products. You will learn not only to recognize and avoid toxic contaminants, but, importantly, how to recognize ingredients and use products that promote healthy skin, perform treatments to reverse damage, and provide world-class service for an experience your future clients will find unforgettable.




Facials are workouts for your skin



Pure Aesthetics offers a wide variety of spa services, performed by our students, at reasonable prices. You can book a custom facial, microdermabrasion, body treatments, waxing, and more. For a detailed list of services, plus specials, please see our menu on the Services page.
