KAELEN H. JOHNSON


FOUNDER.

DIRECTOR.

CEO.

INSTRUCTOR.


Kaelen began her career in the health and wellness industry in 1993, at the spa at Canyon Ranch. Additionally, at this time so also worked at a med spa which included consultations for laser hair removal and corrective makeup. With fifteen years at Canyon Ranch, and more than five years at Loews Ventana Canyon Resort Spa, Kaelen provided a wide variety of services that included herbal wraps, facials, and makeup. She was also an educator and sale representative for the exclusive organic skincare product line Essensa and completed a rigorous apprenticeship with Bliss Spa in New York City. Kaelen holds aesthetics licenses in both Arizona and New York, along with her aesthetics instructor license here in Arizona. She has been an invited speaker at national industry conferences and has written for industry trade publications. Inspired by what she wished she learned during her own beauty school experience, Kaelen opened Pure Aesthetics Natural Skincare School in 2006 to help spread her passion for ecological responsibility and to fill the need for a quality aesthetics school in Tucson. In 2014, Kaelen made her dream a reality when she launched a line of organic skincare products. Based on her recognition that what we put onto our bodies is as important as what we put into our bodies, Kaelen and her product development team spent over a year getting the products Made-Safe Certified. This is a demanding process that ensures products are safe for humans, animals, and the environment. In 2018, Kaelen launched KH Organic Day Spa, where she could utilize her products and hire her top graduates. The spa is in the heart of downtown Tucson at the AC Hotel.



Why choose us?



Pure Aesthetics Natural Skincare School is Tucson’s premier school dedicated exclusively to aesthetics education, providing a high quality skincare education based upon an integrated wellness approach.


Our students learn the latest trends and techniques along with basic skills in a spa-like setting from accomplished professionals with practical experience within the industry. Offered in a positive and creative atmosphere, the training encourages personal growth and produces qualified, employment-ready graduates. After completing their coursework at Pure Aesthetics, our well-informed students can educate even the most savvy and inquisitive client.
