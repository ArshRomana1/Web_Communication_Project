Our standard for skincare products:

No artificial preservatives, colorants or fragrances; no petroleum-based chemicals; no harsh ingredients; no DEA or TEA; no sodium laurel sulfate; no animal testing.

(Please note that because the products we carry contain only natural ingredients, products may vary in color from batch to batch.)
