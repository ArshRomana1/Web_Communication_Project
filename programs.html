120 hour
Advanced Aesthetics
  1. The primary goal of the advanced aesthetics and cosmetic laser technician program is to not only meet but exceed state mandated basic skills training.
  2. Once students complete the 600 required hours for the aesthetic portion of the program, they will begin their 120 hours of training for Laser, Microneedling, Radiofrequency, and Skin Classic.
  3. Pure Aesthetics’ laser program educates students in many areas including but not limited to the anatomy and history of the skin as it relates to aesthetics laser procedures, basic concepts about lasers and laser systems, and the importance of safety and contraindications.

700 hour
Professional Massage Therapy Program
  1. The 700-hour Professional Massage Therapy Program provides students with rigorous, comprehensive training in the art and science of massage therapy.
  2. Coursework includes human anatomy and physiology, kinesiology, pathology, medical terminology, business and ethics, integrative assessment and documentation, and various massage modalities.
  3. The curriculum furnishes a solid foundation to master many different bodywork techniques. Additionally, students will apply their broad theoretical knowledge and developing skills through hands-on experience practicing on clients at the student clinic.
